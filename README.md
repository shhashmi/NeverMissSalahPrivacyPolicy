# Privacy Policy for Never Miss Salah

**Effective Date:** January 20, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 20, 2025

## Introduction

Never Miss Salah ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Precise Location:** We collect your device's GPS coordinates to find nearby mosques and display them on the map.
- **Purpose:** Location data is used solely to:
  - Show your current position on the map
  - Search for mosques within a 5km radius of your location
  - Calculate distances to nearby prayer locations
- **Storage:** Location data is processed in real-time and is NOT stored on our servers. It remains on your device only.

### Google Maps Integration
We use Google Maps Services to:
- Display interactive maps
- Show mosque locations with custom markers
- Search for places using Google Places API
- Provide autocomplete functionality for place searches

Google's privacy practices are governed by their [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect or store personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- We do NOT track your browsing history or app usage patterns
- We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
- We do NOT store your search history
- We do NOT share your location data with third parties (except Google Maps API for functionality)

## Permissions Required

### Location Permission (Required)
- **Android Permissions:** `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`, `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`
- **Purpose:** To determine your current location and find nearby mosques
- **Control:** You can revoke this permission anytime through your device settings, but the app will not function without it

### Internet Permission (Required)
- **Purpose:** To fetch mosque data from Google Places API and display prayer times
- **Note:** Required for core app functionality

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Google Maps Platform
- **Service:** Google Maps SDK, Google Places API
- **Purpose:** Map display, location search, and place information
- **Data Shared:** Your location coordinates (temporarily for API requests)
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Google Play Services
- **Purpose:** Maps, location services, and app infrastructure
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Security

- All API communications are performed over HTTPS
- No sensitive data is stored locally on your device
- Location data is never logged or retained after use
- We implement industry-standard security practices

## Children's Privacy

Never Miss Salah does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children.

## Data Retention

We do NOT retain any user data because:
- Location data is processed in real-time only
- No user accounts or profiles are created
- No personal information is collected

## Your Rights
